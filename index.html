HSE Nexus Manual

This notebook tool allows you to aggregate safety information files and interact with them in real time using Gemini models.

Step 1: Connection & Setup

Input your Gemini API key inside the bar on the top header. Click 'Connect'. A validated key turns the connection indicator Glowing Green.

Step 2: Upload Documents

Upload files (PDF, Word, TXT, or images/scans) through the left panel. Alternatively, load pre-loaded presets like ISO 45001.

Step 3: Build Safety Artifacts

Navigate the center tabs to highlight terms, map relations, model a 5x5 Risk Matrix, or checklist compliance.

Step 4: Ask Gemini

Ask queries. The chatbot will dynamically ground answers in document texts, referencing pages and standard items accurately.
